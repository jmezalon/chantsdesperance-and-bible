Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 30, 2026

Chants d'Espérance & Bible ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Our app collects minimal information to provide you with the best experience:

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your username and email address to enable features like hymn submissions.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app experience.
  • Local Storage: Your preferences, favorites, and reading history are stored locally on your device.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • Allow you to submit hymn contributions
  • Save your preferences and favorites
  • Improve our services

Data Storage and Security

Most of your data (favorites, preferences, reading history) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. Account information, if provided, is stored securely and protected using industry-standard encryption.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Bolls.life API: To provide Bible text content. Please refer to their privacy policy for information on how they handle data.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app contains religious content (hymns and Bible passages) appropriate for family use.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Clear locally stored data through your device settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
